Visit Ethiopia

Visiting Ethiopia offers a journey through rich history, diverse landscapes, and vibrant cultures, from the ancient rock-hewn churches of Lalibela to the modern, bustling capital of Addis Ababa. Travelers can explore the dramatic geography of the Danakil Depression or trek in the Simien Mountains, while also encountering unique traditions of its many ethnic groups and experiencing a different calendar system, which is several years behind the Gregorian calendar.
